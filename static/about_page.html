About FeelzApp.

FeelzApp is your new diary. You can post anonymously
or reveal your identity. Have a strong feeling? Happy? Sad?
Angry? Let it out on the FeelzApp. Did you read a post that
gave you the Feelz? Bump it to the top of the feed so others
can get their Feelz on too. We're all friends here, so be nice!
Enjoy!
